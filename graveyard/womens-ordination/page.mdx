---
title: "Reflections on the Practical Implications of Women's Ordination"
date: 2025-05-07T15:54:22.000Z
---

1. It is more difficult to live the Christian life as a man than as a woman,
   and quite massively so, due to the male concupiscence situation.
2. In times past, Christianity offered men both a greater burden and greater
   authority.
3. Now Christianity offers men a greater burden, but no greater authority.
